Appearance & language

From Settings → Appearance & language you decide how Continuity looks and reads: the interface language, the theme (light, dark or your system's), and the color palette of accent colors. Every change applies instantly and is saved to your account, so it syncs with the mobile app. This guide walks through all four parts with screenshots.

How to use it

  • Open your account menu and go to Settings; in the rail choose Appearance & language (🎨 icon).
  • All three controls —Language, Theme and Palette— live in a single card, in that order.
  • Back to dashboard (top-left) leaves Settings.

How to edit it

  • There's no “Save” button: every selection applies instantly.
  • Each change shows a Saved toast and is stored on your account.
  • Because it's saved to your account, it syncs with the mobile app.

Choosing a theme

Light, dark, the brand theme, or whatever your device uses.

Theme

Choose your preferred appearance. "System" follows your device setting.

▲The active theme stays highlighted. The four options: Continuuit, Light, Dark and System.

How to use it

  • Continuuit is the brand theme and the default.
  • Light / Dark pin the appearance regardless of the time.
  • System follows your device's light/dark setting and switches on its own.

How to edit it

  • Tap an option and the theme changes immediately across the app.
  • It's saved to your account (Saved toast) and syncs to mobile.
  • The theme controls light/dark; accent colors are set separately under Palette.
Theme and palette work together: the theme decides the light or dark background and the palette decides the accent colors on top of it. That's why switching themes updates the palette swatches to preview what you'll actually see.

▲Each button carries two circles with that palette's accent colors. The active one (Default) is highlighted. There are more palettes: Green, Cute, Boho, Complimentary, Retro…

How to use it

  • The palette changes the accents: buttons, links, progress bars and highlights.
  • It doesn't touch the background or text: that's the Theme's job.
  • The two circles preview the accent and secondary color.

How to edit it

  • Tap a palette and the accents change instantly across the app.
  • It's saved to your account (Saved toast) and syncs to mobile.
  • The swatches recompute for the active theme so they reflect the real color.
There are 13 palettes in total. A few are shown above; scroll the row to see the rest (Green, Cute, Boho, Complimentary, Retro).

Choosing a language

Spanish or English for the whole interface.

▲The active language (English) is highlighted. The two options: English and Español.

How to use it

  • Changes the language of the entire app interface.
  • It also affects the digests and notifications you receive by email.

How to edit it

  • Tap English or Español; the app re-renders immediately in that language.
  • It's saved to your account (Saved toast) and syncs to mobile.
