# Today's View

The **Today** tab is the first thing you see when you open Continuity. It gathers, in one place, what deserves your attention *today*: tasks coming due, projects going cold, what you've already finished, and the projects that are one step away from closing.

---

## 1. General anatomy

[image: today-01-anatomy.png]

Top to bottom, Today is built like this:

1. **Header** — today's date, a greeting based on the time, and the gear for *Customize Today*.
2. **Attention sections** — alerts and focus: what's urgent stays on top.
3. **Progress sections** — “Done today”, “Close to the finish line”, active/launched projects.
4. **Create button (FAB)** — floating bottom-right, to add a task/project/routine/idea.

> **Note:** sections **only appear when they have data**. If you have no stalled projects, the “Stalled alert” won't show; as you create your first project more blocks gradually appear. It's normal for a new user to see only a few sections at first.

---

## 2. Header & greeting

[image: today-02-header.png]

Your orientation point when you open the app. The greeting changes: **Good morning** (5–12h), **Good afternoon** (12–19h), **Good evening** (rest).

**How to use it**
- The date and greeting are automatic; they aren't editable.
- The gear ⚙ opens **Customize Today** (section 13).
- On mobile the greeting sits in the header; on desktop the gear appears at the top-right.

---

## 3. Counters *(mobile only)*

[image: today-03-counters.png]

A quick number strip: active, launched, stalled, ideas and tasks. Scrolls horizontally.

**How to use it**
- A quick snapshot of your workspace's overall state.
- Values are computed automatically from your projects, ideas and tasks.

**How to edit it**
- It's a **hideable** section. You can hide it from *Customize Today*.
- It currently shows **on mobile only** (it carries the *Mobile* tag in the editor).

---

## 4. Stalled alert

[image: today-04-stalled.png]

Projects with no activity in 7+ days — so they don't quietly die. Each chip shows the name and the days idle.

**How to use it**
- **Tap a chip** to jump straight to that project and revive it.
- Appears only if you have stalled projects (≥7 days without movement).

**How to edit it**
- **Hideable** section from *Customize Today*.
- To “clear” a project from here: open it and log an update or move a task forward.

---

## 5. Today's Focus *(always visible)*

[image: today-05-focus.png]

The heart of the view: what actually needs doing today. Labels mark priority: **Overdue**, **Due today**, **Stalled** and **Next step**.

**How to use it**
- Click the ✓ circle on the left to mark the task done.
- Click the task text to open its editor.
- Click the project name to jump to it.
- “View all tasks” opens the Tasks tab with the rest.

**How to edit it**
- It's the **only section that cannot be hidden** (shown with 🔒 in the editor).
- You can still **move** it in *Customize Today*.
- Its content is filled from: dated tasks, project next steps, and stalled projects.

> If nothing is urgent you'll see **“Nothing pressing today.”** with a hint to add your first project or next steps. That means you're on top of things, not that something is broken.

---

## 6. Routines for today

[image: today-06-routines.png]

Your recurring routines due today (plus overdue ones from the last 14 days).

**How to use it**
- **Complete** marks today's occurrence as done (it moves to “Done today”).
- The chip on the right shows how many are due today and how many overdue, and links to the Routines tab.
- Tap a routine to edit it.

**How to edit it**
- **Hideable** section from *Customize Today*.
- The routines themselves are created/edited from the **Routines** tab or the Create button.

---

## 7. Done today

[image: today-07-done.png]

Your record of the day: completed tasks, routines and log entries. Filter with the “N tasks” / “N entries” chips to see just one type.

**How to use it**
- Automatically gathers what you completed today.
- **Undo** (↩) reverts a task/routine you checked by mistake.
- Shows hours worked and a breakdown by project.

**How to edit it**
- **Hideable** section; it starts **collapsed** by default (tap the title to open it).
- The filter chips are a *toggle*: tap again to see everything.

---

## 8. Close to the finish line

[image: today-08-closeable.png]

Nearly-finished projects (“Almost there”) and ones you can close with few tasks (“Quick win”).

**How to use it**
- **Tap a card** to open the project and give it the final push.
- The progress bar reflects done / total tasks.

**How to edit it**
- **Hideable** section from *Customize Today*.
- It appears on its own when projects qualify.

---

## 9. Sleeping projects

[image: today-09-sleeping.png]

Projects going cold, color-coded by severity: **7–14d** → **15–30d** → **30d+**.

**How to use it**
- **“Resume”** opens the update log to reactivate the project.
- Tap the name to open the project directly.
- If it has a next step, you'll see it under the name (→ …).

**How to edit it**
- **Hideable** section from *Customize Today*.

---

## 10. Stale ideas

[image: today-10-ideas.png]

Ideas that have been waiting 30+ days for a decision. It's a single block that takes you to the Ideas tab when tapped.

**How to use it**
- **Tap the block** to go to Ideas and decide: promote to project or drop it.
- Appears only if you have ideas 30+ days old.

**How to edit it**
- **Hideable** section from *Customize Today*.

---

## 11. Active projects · Launched with tasks

[image: today-11-active.png]

Compact cards for your in-progress projects and launched ones that still have pending work. Same card: progress, hours, and “comeback” after a pause.

**How to use it**
- **Tap a card** to open the project.
- They show task progress, total hours and today's hours.

**How to edit it**
- Both are **hideable** and start **collapsed**.
- Handy to hide if you prefer managing projects from the **Projects** tab.

---

## 12. Create button (FAB)

[image: today-12-create.png]

The floating **+** button to add things without leaving Today. Tapping it opens the sheet with the 4 creation actions.

**How to use it**
- A universal shortcut to create a **task, project, routine or idea**.
- On desktop the Create button sits alongside access to **Loop** (the AI assistant).

**How to edit it**
- The FAB **can't be hidden** or reordered: it's a fixed part of the view.
- It disappears while you're in *Customize Today* mode.

---

## 13. Customize Today

Reorder, hide and show sections however you like. This is the “edit” for the whole view.

**Step A — Enter the editor:** tap the gear ⚙ in the header. (You also land here from the last onboarding step.)

[image: today-13-customize.png]

Each row is one section. Drag **⠿** to reorder; the **eye** 👁 shows/hides; the **lock** 🔒 marks the ones that can't be hidden.

**Editor actions**
- Drag a row's **⠿** handle to change the order (keyboard works too).
- Tap the **eye** 👁 to hide a section; tap again to show it. Hidden ones show a dashed border and a *Hidden* tag.
- Rows with a **lock** 🔒 (e.g. “Today's Focus”) can't be hidden, but can be moved.
- The *Mobile* tag marks sections that currently show on phones only.

**Save & reset**
- **Done** (green button) or the **✕** exit the editor and save your layout.
- **Reset** ↩ returns the default order and visibility.
- On exit, Today reloads to reflect what you showed/hid.
- Your setup is saved to your account (it syncs with the mobile app).

**Hidden-sections footer**

[image: today-14-customize.png]

When you have hidden sections, this footer appears at the bottom of Today and reminds you how many, with a shortcut to the editor.
