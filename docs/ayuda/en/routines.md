# Routines

The **Routines** tab gathers your *recurring* activities: things that repeat daily, weekly, every N days, or on a fixed day of the month. Each routine produces one **occurrence** per date; completing today's occurrence records it, and the routine reappears when it's next due.

---

## 1. The routines list

[image: routines-01-overview.png]

Your routines are grouped by when they're due, with a search box on top. Each block is **collapsible**: tap the title to open or close.

1. **Today & overdue** — occurrences due today plus the overdue ones (up to 14 days). Open by default.
2. **Completed today** — what you've already marked done today, in case you want to undo it.
3. **Upcoming (7 days)** — what's coming in the next week.
4. **Later** — one row per routine with its next future occurrence.
5. **Archived** — paused routines that don't generate occurrences.

> **Note:** blocks **only appear when they hold routines**. When you **search**, every block opens and filters by title at once.

---

## 2. Anatomy of a row

[image: routines-02-row.png]

Everything a routine shows. The border tints: **red** if overdue, **orange** if due today.

**How to use it**
- **✓ circle** on the left: complete / undo the occurrence.
- Tap anywhere on the row to **edit it**.
- A **⟲ frequency** chip and a **⏱ effort** chip (hours), if you set one.
- Second line: date (or “Today”), the project with its color dot, and the description.

**How to edit it**
- **✎ Edit** opens the routine in its modal.
- **🗄 Archive** pauses it without deleting its history.
- **✕ Delete** removes it entirely.
- On desktop these icons appear on hover; on mobile they're always visible.

---

## 3. Recurrence & frequency

[image: routines-03-recurrence.png]

The frequency chip sums up how often a routine repeats and is derived automatically from the type you picked.

**The four types**
- **Once** — happens on a single date; doesn't repeat.
- **Weekly (days)** — pick weekdays (M · T · W · T · F · S · S).
- **Every N…** — every N days, weeks or months.
- **Day of month** — a fixed day (e.g. the 1st) each month.

**How they spread across the list**
- Occurrences due today or overdue (up to 14 days) land in **Today & overdue**.
- Those within the next 7 days go to **Upcoming**.
- If the next date falls beyond that, the routine shows once in **Later**.

---

## 4. Create & edit

[image: routines-04-create.png]

The **New** button (or tapping a row to edit) opens the routine modal. The fields change with the recurrence type: here “Weekly” shows the weekday picker.

**How to use it**
- Add a **title** (required) and, optionally, a short description.
- Link the routine to a **project** to see it with that color and count its effort there.
- Choose the **recurrence type**; depending on it you'll see weekdays, an interval, or a day of month.
- Set the **effort** in hours so the ⏱ chip shows on the row.

**How to edit it**
- **Tap a row** (or its ✎) to reopen this same modal, now titled *Edit routine*.
- You can also create a routine from the **Create (+) button** in any view.
- Non-“Once” recurrences accept an optional **end date**.

---

## 5. Completing today's

[image: routines-05-complete.png]

Mark today's occurrence; it's recorded and you can undo it. On complete a **“✓ Routine completed”** toast appears and the row moves to *Completed today*.

**How to use it**
- Tap the **✓ circle** on the left of the row to mark it done.
- The occurrence moves to the **Completed today** block with strikethrough text.
- It also shows under **“Done today”** on the Today tab.

**How to edit it**
- Tap the **circle** of an already-completed routine again to **undo**.
- Handy if you checked one by mistake: it returns to “Today & overdue”.

---

## 6. Archived & empty state

[image: routines-06-archived.png]

Pause routines you don't use; archived ones generate no occurrences.

[image: routines-07-archived.png]

Before you have routines you'll see the empty state with a shortcut to create the first one.

**How to use it**
- **🗄 Archive** a routine to remove it from the active blocks without deleting its history.
- The **Archived** block appears only if you have archived ones.
- **↺ Restore** brings it back and resumes generating occurrences.

**How to edit it**
- **✕ Delete** (on active or archived) removes the routine entirely.
- The **search box** also filters within Archived by title.
